Jordan J. Dominguez

Designer | Developer | Photographer


This is the story of Jordan Dominguez...


Jordan is a San Diego-bred, business-savvy, soon-to-be full-stack Javascript developer with 3 years of experience in the tech industry. His original background is in digital marketing strategy and graphic design, but he has newly-discovered a passion for web development.

Professional Experience

A cum laude graduate of California Polytechnic State University San Luis Obispo, Jordan holds a degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing Management and a minor in Psychology.

Prior to switching into the web development world, he worked as a Digital Marketing Consultant at Unanimous AI, a Silicon Valley technology company that is currently building the next generation of artificial swarm intelligence software.

Before that, he was a Digital Marketing Strategist at The Townsend Team marketing firm, which is a San Diego-based firm specializing in science and technology companies. There he accumulated a wide range of experience working on campaigns spanning from the national launch of several consumer products to a number of large-scale PR campaigns for multimillion-dollar corporations (Peregrine Semiconductor and CentrexIT).

Hobbies and Lifestyle

Aside from development and digital advertising work, he is a published photographer who enjoys exploring new places with camera in hand. Born and raised in Southern California, on the weekends you can either find him at the beach enjoying the San Diego sun or creating something new.
